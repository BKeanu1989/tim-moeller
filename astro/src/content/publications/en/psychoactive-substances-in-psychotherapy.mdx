This literature review examines the use of psilocybin in psychotherapeutic treatment for mental illnesses, specifically focusing on studies published between 2017 and 2022. The review suggests that psilocybin shows promise in treating tobacco addiction and anxiety/depression related to life-threatening illnesses, with observed clinical improvements.
